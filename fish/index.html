Fish on Diet

STORY:

It's the story about "Lady Pink Fish", who wants to keep fit and look better. But on her way to "Green Gate" everyday, she cannot help eating more food (white circle) to turn fatter, but also gets more exercise(yellow circle)to become thinner. Also, some other obstacles(colorful rectangular and circle) will block her sight. Only the right and health shape she has can help her to go through the final "green gate". That's the life!

INSTRUCTION:

Lady Pink fish movement:
Arrow up, down, left, right

"p": pause

"q": quit

"r": play again

"SpaceBar": play next Level
