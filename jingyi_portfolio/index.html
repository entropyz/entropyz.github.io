Who Am I




Hey everyone! I'm Jingyi Feng :D
I'm so excited to release this new website that I just built!

I'm currently a User Experience designer working at Great NYC area. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University's Entertainment Technology Center (a "mixed" graduate program hold by both Computer science and Design schools), focusing on product design and creating UX solutions. I worked on the interdisciplinary projects for organizations like Electronic Arts, Google and DARPA, helping them mold their products/technology with "human centered" design insights. Prior to coming to the U.S, I worked in Alibaba Group in China, and received my B.S. degree in Industrial Design from Zhejiang University.

I like observing people, and listening to their stories.
I love to discover the world, and seek the beauty in ordinary things.
I feel happiest when I come up with creative solutions to the problems.

Besides those great things, I enjoy skiing, hiking, canoeing, reading, cooking, listening to diverse music, visiting unknown but great museums, exploring new terrain, and,,, I love puppies!
