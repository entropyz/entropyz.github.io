Who Am I




Hey everyone! I'm Jingyi Feng :D
I'm so excited to release this new website that I just built!

I'm currently a User Experience designer working at FinTech industry in great NYC area. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University's Entertainment Technology Center (which is a "mixed" master program hold by both Computer science and Design schools), mainly focusing on product design and creative UX solutions. I've worked on a number of interdisciplinary projects for different organizations, like Electronic Arts, DARPA and Google, helping mold the products/technology with "human centered" design insights. Prior to coming to the U.S., I interned at Alibaba in China, and received my B.S. degree in Industrial Design from Zhejiang University.

When away from the working desk, I love skiing, hiking, canoeing, reading, cooking, listening to diverse music, visiting unknown but interesting museums, and exploring new terrain!
