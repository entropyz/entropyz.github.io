Boiok

App Design, UX, Mobile

Creativity comes from everyday experience
Design Brief

Boiok is one of my side projects aiming to provide a more interesting and greener way for bike rental experience. It helps people easily rent a bike based on their various needs. This mobile app design mainly focuses on seeking, retrieving and organizing information in support of people’s rental and cycling experience.

I started this project because of the days I lived in SF bay area. I hoped I could find an easy way to bike in this beautiful area, for commutes, trips, or just for fun.
