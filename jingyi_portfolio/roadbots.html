RoadBots

Product Design, UX, Mobile

Gamifying User Experience Design
Design Brief

Designing RoadBots is a great experience I’ve ever had. My team, Charles Aweida, Yanjin Long, Anshul Goyal, and I, we were so dedicated to this project for almost one month, going through the entire process - early research, marketing analysis, device study, ideation, wireframing, prototyping, and later implementation. This is a project that is all about product design.

Basically it’s an idea about seeking a creative solution for safe driving. We would like to make it functional, rewarded, and super cool. Taking two user sides (customers and clients) into account, we tried to build a real ecosystem that makes the whole driving experience safe based on strong motivation and good incentive. Simply put, we were gamifying something with a real payoff.

It was a really exciting project. Our friends like this product a lot. Hopefully one day we can bring this business model to life.

Research



We interviewed with 13 commuters, conducted surveys did the Internet research and compared the existing apps. We found: driving is dangerous Vehicular accidents are the #1 cause of death among young adults Last year 3,331 people were killed in crashes involving a distracted driver 11% of all drivers under the age of 20 involved in fatal crashes were reported as distracted at the time of the crash insurance is expensive Average cost of car insurance in 2012 was $1428 (California) 20% of insurance customers experienced an insurance initiated rate increase from 2011 Of those customers 63% experienced an increase of $50 or more games? Maybe it’s worthwhile to try to encourage users and solve the problem in use of game thinking or some game mechanics

How do we do this? A creative solution for driving, making it safe but rewarded and with a lot fun. How does it work? Progressive snapshot vs. mobile device

Ideation

Meet with our RoadBots Team:






Each road bot records one kind of your driving data, and sends it to your insurance company. You will also earn road bots points by having the them record your data. You can select which data you want the road bots to send to your insurance company when you sign up. You can change your preferences later too.




Team up and win the game:




You can earn road bot points for your whole insurance company, your community and your state. The higher total scores your teams earn, the more discount you can get for your insurance too.
